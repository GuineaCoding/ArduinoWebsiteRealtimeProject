Home Sphere

Home Sphere is an innovative application that brings together the power of Firebase, the versatility of the Ionic framework, and the precision of Arduino-based sensor data. It's designed to provide seamless home automation and real-time monitoring, enhancing the way you interact with your environment. Experience intuitive control and insightful data visualization at your fingertips.

Temperature tracker

Monitor ambient temperature with precision. Ideal for ensuring comfortable living environments and managing heating systems efficiently.

Humidity tracker

Keep track of humidity levels for optimal comfort and health. Essential for preventing mold growth and maintaining air quality.

Moisture tracker

Detect soil moisture levels for effective plant care or leak detection. A valuable tool for gardeners and homeowners alike.

Pressure Tracker

Measure atmospheric pressure for various applications, including weather forecasting and altitude monitoring.

Pir Sensor Tracker

Enhance security with passive infrared (PIR) motion detection. Ideal for automatic lighting, alarm systems, and occupancy sensing.

Air Quality Tracker

Keep an eye on air pollutants and maintain healthy indoor air quality. Critical for comfort and reducing health risks.
